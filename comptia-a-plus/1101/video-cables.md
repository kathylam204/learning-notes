# Video Cables
## VGA (Video Graphics Array)
- DB-15 connector
- blue color
- video only (no audio)
- analog
- image degrades after 5 to 10 meters
## HDMI (High-Definition Multimedia Interface)
- video and audio stream
- digital
- about a 20 meter maximum cable distance
- 19-pin connector
## DisplayPort
- sends digital information in packetized form (like ethernet and PCIe)
- both audio and video
- mini displayport variety available
- compatible with HDMI and DVI, using an adapter
## DVI (Digital Video Interface)
- single link video: 3.7 Gb/s
- dual link video: 7.4 Gb/s
- no audio support
