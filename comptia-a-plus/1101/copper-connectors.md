# Copper Connectors
## RJ11 Connector
- used for a telephone or DSL connection
- 6 position, 2 conductors (6P2C), though some cables may need additional conductors
## RJ45 Connector
- registered jack type 45
- 8 position, 8 conductors (8P2C)
- modular
- commonly used for ethernet
## F-Connector
- used on a coaxial cable
- screws in
- used for cable television, cable modems, or for DOCIS
## Molex Connector
- 4-pin power connector
- provides +12V and +5V
- provides power to many devices, particularly storage devices and fans
