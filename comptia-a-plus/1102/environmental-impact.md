# Environmental Impacts
## Material Safety Data Sheets (acronym)
- MSDS
## MSDS
- required by OSHA
- may be abbreviated as SDS
- provides information for all hazardous chemicals
## Handling Toxic Waste (Batteries/Toner)
- Batteries
- usually sent to a hazardous waste facility
- Toner
- recycle and reuse
## Humidity (High/Low)
- high humidity promotes condensation
- low humidity promotes static discharges
- 50% humidity is a good number
## Uninterruptible Power Supply (UPS) Types (term-5list)
- standby UPS
- line-interactive UPS
- on-line UPS
## Surge Suppressor
- filters power spikes and line noise
- spikes are diverted to the ground
## Surge Suppressor Specifications (list)
- joule ratings
- surge absorption
- look for 600+ joules of protection
- surge amp ratings
- UL 1449 voltage let-through ratings
- ratings at 500, 400, and 300
- lower is better
