# Document Types
## Acceptable Use Policy (acronym)
- AUP
## Acceptable Use Policy
- detailed documentation of the acceptable use of company assets
- limits legal liability
## Network Topology Diagram
- logical diagram of the network layout
## Compliance
- meeting the standards of laws, policies, and regulations
- a healthy catalog of rules
## Splash Screens
- a message, logo, or graphic shown during startup or login
- can be used for branding or to require compliance
